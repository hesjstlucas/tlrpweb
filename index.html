Rules

The standard is simple: stay believable and respect the scene.

TLRP is built around clean interactions. The point is not to overcomplicate roleplay, it is to make sure the people around you can trust the scene to stay fair, grounded, and enjoyable.

01

Respect the scene

Give other players room to contribute and do not turn every interaction into a one-person moment.

02

Keep actions realistic

Decisions, reactions, and character behavior should fit the situation instead of breaking immersion.

03

Follow staff direction

When staff step in, respond quickly and move forward so the rest of the server is not held up.
