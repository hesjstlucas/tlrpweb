Rules

Standards that protect the scene and the people in it.

Tallahassee City Roleplay keeps its rules simple on purpose. The goal is not to bury players in text. It is to create a server where scenes stay believable, people understand the standard, and staff can keep the city moving without confusion.

What matters Respect the scene. Stay realistic. Listen when staff step in.
01

Respect ongoing scenes

Do not force yourself into every interaction. Let scenes develop naturally and give others room to contribute.

02

Keep actions believable

Character choices, reactions, and outcomes should fit the situation instead of breaking immersion for quick wins.

03

Use common sense

If something would obviously damage the quality of the roleplay around you, stop and take the better route.

04

Follow staff direction

When moderation steps in, respond quickly and calmly so the situation can be resolved without dragging out the problem.

05

Do not scene ruin

Random disruption, constant trolling, and careless actions that derail roleplay will not help you here.

06

Keep communication respectful

Whether you are in game or in Discord, the baseline is the same: talk to people like you want the community to last.

Need updates?
